Our Story

Why We Built
Blue Eye & Associates

Every founder has felt it — the moment when you need a lawyer, an accountant, a brand designer, and a digital strategist all at once, but you're bouncing between four different agencies, paying for coordination that never actually happens.

That was the gap we saw. Not a small one. A canyon.

The Problem We Lived

India's startup ecosystem is one of the most dynamic in the world — but the professional services supporting it are fragmented by design. A CA firm doesn't do branding. A law firm won't touch your Google Ads. A marketing agency has no idea what FEMA compliance means. Founders are forced to manage five vendors, five invoices, five different people who don't talk to each other — and somewhere in that gap, things fall through.

We've seen companies miss ROC deadlines because their accountant didn't know the lawyer had restructured the shareholding. We've seen brands built without any thought of trademark protection. We've seen websites go live with zero SEO foundation. These aren't rare failures — they're routine.

"A founder shouldn't have to choose between building their business and managing their compliance. We built Blue Eye so they don't have to."

Why Blue Eye

Blue Eye & Associates was founded by a Chartered Accountant and entrepreneur with advanced legal training through LLB studies — three disciplines under one perspective, by design. We are CA-qualified, legally informed, and startup-minded. We understand P&L anxiety. We understand regulatory risk. And we understand that a brand is not just a logo — it is a promise.

The "Third Eye" isn't a metaphor — it's our operating principle. Most businesses see what's directly in front of them. We're trained to see what's coming, what's been missed, and what's quietly holding them back. That diagnostic clarity is what we bring to every engagement — before we execute a single deliverable.

What We're Building

Our mission is to be the default professional partner for every ambitious founder in India — from Day 1 of incorporation through Series A and beyond. We're strengthening the roots of the startup ecosystem, one founder at a time: getting their legal foundation right, their financial reporting clean, their brand sharp, and their digital presence working for them while they sleep.

We're headquartered in Hyderabad with eyes across India and cross-border expertise in the US. And we're just getting started.

When you fill our consultation or enquiry forms, we collect your name, phone number, email address, and the details you share about your business or query. We also collect basic technical data automatically — your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

Why we collect it

Solely to respond to your enquiry, schedule your consultation, and deliver the services you engage us for. We also maintain records as required by Indian law. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for commercial purposes — ever.

Where it's stored

Form submissions are stored in a secured Google Workspace account accessible only by authorised Firm personnel. Our website is hosted on Vercel with full SSL encryption. We do not store your payment card details.

How long we keep it

Enquiries with no follow-on engagement: 12 months. Active client records: duration of engagement + 7 years, as required under the Income Tax Act, 1961 and Companies Act, 2013.

Under India's DPDP Act 2023, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. Email us at info@blueeyeandassociates.in — we respond within 30 days.

We use only essential cookies required for the site to function. No advertising cookies, no retargeting, no third-party tracking of any kind.
